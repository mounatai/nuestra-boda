Things to do in Blanes

A few ideas for beach days, nature, family activities, culture, and day trips nearby.

🌊 Beach & Nature

🏖️ Platja de Blanes

The main beach with fine sand, calm water, restaurants and play areas for kids.

🏖️ Platja de S’Abanell

Long, spacious beach with sports areas, promenade and lots of space for families.

🏖️ Cala Sant Francesc (Cala Bona)

A small, beautiful cove with pine trees—great for swimming and snorkeling.

🏖️ Cala Treumal

A quieter, more natural cove north of Blanes—perfect for relaxed beach days.

🌿 Jardí Botànic Marimurtra

Stunning botanical garden with spectacular views over the Mediterranean Sea.

🌅 Sa Palomera

The iconic rock at the start of the Costa Brava—especially beautiful at sunset.

🚶‍♀️ Promenade & bike path

Perfect for walking, jogging or cycling right by the sea.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Activities with kids

🐬 Marineland Catalunya (Palafolls)

Water park + animal park with dolphins, sea lions and kid-friendly areas.

🎠 Gnomo Park (Lloret de Mar)

Family park with mini-golf, trampolines and play areas.

💦 Water World (Lloret de Mar)

Large water park with slides and pools for all ages.

🌊 Stand-up paddle & kayak

Rentals available directly at several beaches.

🤿 Snorkeling & boat tours

Including glass-bottom boats—great for families.

🚤 Pedal boats & Banana Boat

Classic water fun for kids and adults alike.

🏛️ Culture & Discoveries

🏰 Castell de Sant Joan

Castle with breathtaking panoramic views over Blanes.

🏘️ Old town of Blanes

Small streets, local shops and weekly markets.

🎆 International Fireworks Competition

Summer event (usually in July).

🎶 Local markets & festivals

Depending on the season: music, food and cultural programs.

🚗 Day trips nearby

🏝️ Tossa de Mar

Romantic coastal town with a historic walled old town.

🛍️ Sant Feliu de Guíxols & Platja d’Aro

Perfect for a day trip.

🎨 Cadaqués & Cap de Creus

Beautiful nature and artsy region for a longer outing.

🍽️ Food & Enjoy

🍴 Restaurants & beach bars

Along the seafront promenade—perfect for a relaxed meal by the sea.

🍦 Ice cream & crêperies

For a sweet afternoon or an evening stroll.

🥕 Local markets

Fresh produce for picnics at the beach or in the park.

Tip: Many places are easy to reach on foot — for day trips, renting a car or taking a taxi is useful.
